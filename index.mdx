## No more re-reading files. No more re-explaining architecture.

TokenStash is a Claude Code plugin that saves summaries of your project to `.tokenstash/` as you work. At the start of every session, the stash is automatically injected into Claude's context — so Claude remembers your codebase, your decisions, and your patterns.

## What gets stashed

TokenStash maintains four types of context files:

- **architecture.md** — High-level codebase overview, structure, patterns, and key components
- **decisions.md** — Decision log documenting what was decided each session and why
- **rules.md** — Project-specific rules and conventions (like CLAUDE.md on steroids)
- **files/\<path\>.md** — Per-file summaries (3-5 lines each) that update automatically
